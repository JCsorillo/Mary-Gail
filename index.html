About Us

Welcome to Traveler

Hey everyone! I'm Mary Gail Valeriano, a student at NISU's College of Information and Computing Studies. While I'm busy studying, I also have a huge passion for exploring the world! That's why I created Traveler, a website dedicated to sharing my adventures and inspiring others to travel.

On Traveler, you'll find tons of information about popular destinations like the Philippines, Korea, Japan, the USA, and France. I'll share my personal experiences, tips for planning your trips, and recommendations for the best things to see and do in each place.

I'm a big fan of exploring new places, trying new foods, and experiencing different cultures. I love sharing my travel stories and helping others plan their own adventures.

So, if you're looking for a travel website that's fun, informative, and filled with my personal experiences, then check out Traveler! Let's explore the world together!
