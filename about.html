Welcome to Traveler

Hey everyone! I'm Mary Gail Valeriano, a student at NISU's College of Information and Computing Studies. While I'm busy studying, I also have a huge passion for exploring the world! It's not just about the destinations, it's about the experiences, the cultures, the food, the people, and the memories. That's why I created Traveler, a website dedicated to sharing my adventures and inspiring others to travel.

On Traveler, you'll find tons of information about popular destinations like the Philippines, Korea, Japan, the USA, and France. I'm not just sharing travel guides; I'm sharing my personal experiences, the things that made me laugh, the moments that took my breath away, and the things that made me realize how much beauty there is in the world. You'll also find tips for planning your trips, recommendations for the best things to see and do in each place, and maybe even a few hidden gems that I stumbled upon along the way.

Besides my love for travel, I'm also a big fan of anime and K-dramas, playing sports like table tennis, basketball, volleyball, and Arnis, dancing, trying new foods, and getting lost in a good manga. These hobbies not only bring me joy but also inspire me to explore different cultures and perspectives.

I believe that travel has the power to change you, to broaden your horizons, and to make you a more open-minded and compassionate person.

So, if you're looking for a travel website that's fun, informative, and filled with my personal experiences, then check out Traveler! Let's explore the world together!
